About Us

Thank you for checking out Cat Crescent Embroidery! We are a small company based Arizona that was started because of the owners blossoming love of embroidery. “I saw some amazing pieces and immediately wanted to learn everything I could.” Here we offer many interesting and unusual embroidery pieces. Each of our items is lovingly stitched by hand and comes directly from the owner’s mind without a pattern. And you can trust that every item is handled with care.

Please enjoy our selection!
