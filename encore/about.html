About Us

Encore Enterprises Tax Service started in 1985 in Detroit Michigan. We started as 3 employees: 2 CPA’s and 1 Accountant. We have grown to over 25 employees and 4 different states.

Encore is a full-service Accounting firm. We handle all aspects of business accounting. From Accounts Payable, Accounts receivable, and payroll. We can get it done, regardless of the company size.

We are best known for our Excellent Tax Service. We prepare and Efile all states and federal income tax returns.

We can complete the process online. No need to travel to an office. With today’s technology of “Google Meet”. Your appointment can be handled with your smartphone.
