About

He is my SS Agent or servant (his words not mine) and he is descended from the evil nine-tailed fox. His family kept him under house arrest because they feared the powers of the kitsune, and in order to obtain my freedom he learned to manipulate and appeal to others through obeying their whims.

However, because of this, he was unable to form his own personality until he started corresponding with me through letters, under the guise of my fiance Kagerō. Acorrding to him, he completely devoted to me, will blindly obeying me and protecting me with his life if the situation calls for it.
