Hi

I'm Brittany E. Carter, a coding adventurer, an open-minded conversationalist, amateur photographer, a bit of a risk taker, and a lover of cats. What’s life if not an opportunity to learn by doing something you never thought possible? This thought has given me a thirst for new experiences everyday.

My web development journey started with the impulsive decision to try a coding class. Coding lead me to discover my love of creating and watching ideas evolve from their simple inceptions.

After this I began challenging internships with companies both big and small. My favorite projects have been ones that give others the opportunity to learn and get excited about a subject. It is my hope to continue to create websites that are not only easy to use but also allow for the growth of a community! Take my hand and let me show you something cool.
