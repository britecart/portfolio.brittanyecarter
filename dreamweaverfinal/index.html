Welcome!

Tokyo Ghoul is a Japanese anime and manga that follows Ken Kaneki, a college student who barely survives a deadly encounter with a ghoul named Rize Kamishiro. He is taken to the hospital in critical condition. After recovering, Kaneki discovers that his surgery placed some Rize's organs into his body transforming him into a half-ghoul. Now, like normal ghoul, he must consume human flesh to survive. The ghouls who manage the coffee shop "Anteiku" take him in and teach him to deal with his new life as a half-ghoul. This series show his daily struggles include fitting into the ghoul society, as well as keeping his identity hidden from his human companions. Watch it all unfold on Funimation now!
