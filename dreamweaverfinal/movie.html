Tokyo Ghoul Movie

Tokyo Ghoul live action movie is a Japanese dark fantasy action horror film based on the manga series Tokyo Ghoul by Sui Ishida. The film is directed by Kentarō Hagiwara and stars Masataka Kubota as Ken Kaneki and Fumika Shimizu as Tōuka Kirishima. It was released in Japan by Shochiku on 29 July 2017. This movie focuses on Kaneki's new life as a half-ghoul and stuggle to keep his identity a secret. This movie was released August 10, 2017 recieving a 6.5 out of 10 on rottentomatos.com.
